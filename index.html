Calling Out Bluff: Evaluating Automatic Scoring

NLP, Deep Learning

E- rater systems for answer scoring are gaining popularity due to the vast number of students appearing for examinations such as GRE/ TOEFEL etc. all over the world. However, it is vital to ensure that this E- raters are scoring the answers with precision close to that of a human annotator. If the E-raters are not performing in a similar manner, they may compromise the scores of multiple students, creating a bias in scores. This project aims to verify the accuracy of such State of Art Models by feeding them adversarial samples of answered responses by the students, in a black-box setting.
